Feature Choice Predicts Everything

High Performers:
  • Budgeting Tool: 88% on-time, 0% churn
  • Investment Advice: 88% on-time, 6% churn
  • Savings Tracker: 0% churn, consistent late payment
Danger Zone:
  • Loan Services: 0% on-time, 100% churn

  • Both destroy business value
